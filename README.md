# superstore-analysis

## Business Objective
To analyze sales, profit, and customer performance using historical superstore data and
identify actionable insights to improve profitability and regional performance.

## Dataset
- Source: Superstore sales dataset
- Records: ~9,000
- Key fields: Order Date, Region, Category, Sub-Category, Sales, Profit, Customer Segment

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel (Data cleaning, preprocessing)
- SQL (Joins, aggregations, KPI analysis)
- Power BI (Interactive dashboards & visualization)

## Key Analysis Performed
- Sales and profit trend analysis
- Top and bottom performing products
- Region-wise and category-wise profitability
- Identification of loss-making segments

## KPIs Tracked
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin %
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Regional Contribution

## Business Insights
- Certain sub-categories consistently generated high sales but negative profit.
- West and Central regions showed higher profit margins compared to other regions.
- Discount levels strongly impacted profitability across categories.

## Outcome
The analysis enables management to:
- Optimize discount strategies
- Focus on high-margin products
- Improve regional sales planning
